North Carolina Pulled Pork

The perfect sandwich for the family barbecue

This is the perfect meal to make for large family gatherings!

All you need is a slow cooker and a few other ingredients to impress everyone in your family.

Ingredients

  • 2 pound pork shoulder roast
  • salt and ground black pepper to taste
  • 1/2 cup ketchup
  • 1/2 cup brown sugar
  • 1/3 cup red wine vinegar

Recipe Instructions

  1. Preheat slow cooker on low for 15 minutes
  2. Season pork shoulder with salt and pepper; place pork in preheated slow cooker
  3. Mix ketchup, brown sugar, and vinegar in a bowl; pour over pork
  4. Cook on low for 8 hours. Transfer pork to a large platter and slice into 3 to 4 pieces.
  5. Shred meat with 2 forks and return to slow cooker. Continue to cook for 1 hour
  6. Remove from slow cooker and enjoy
