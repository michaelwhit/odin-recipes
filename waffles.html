Classic Waffles

\

A classic breakfast item made perfect!

In our humble opinion the superior breakfast item.

With just a few simple ingredients you can wow your entire family with your breakfast making abilities

Ingredients

  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 4 teaspoons of baking powder
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 1/2 cups warm milk
  • 1/3 cup butter, melted
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract

Recipe Instructions

  1. In a large bowl, mix together flour, salt, baking powder and sugar; set aside.
  2. Preheat waffle iron to desired temperature.
  3. In a separate bowl, beat the eggs. Stir in the milk, butter and vanilla.
  4. Pour the milk mixture into the flour mixture; beat until blended.
  5. Ladle the batter into a preheated waffle iron.
  6. Cook the waffles until golden and crisp. Serve immediately.
